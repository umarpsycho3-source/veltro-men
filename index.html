About

The Story Behind VELTRO

👨‍💻
Est. 2025 · Ulapane, SL

Hi, I'm Umar — a software engineering student currently completing my 1st year degree, and a passionate graphic designer with a strong eye for aesthetics and visual storytelling.


VELTRO MEN was born from my dual passion for fashion and design. I wanted to build a brand that doesn't just sell clothes — but crafts an identity. Every piece in our collection is hand-picked to reflect confidence, class, and contemporary style for the Sri Lankan man.


From creating custom graphic designs on Fiverr to sourcing premium quality fashion pieces, I bring a designer's eye to every product. VELTRO is more than a store — it's a lifestyle statement.
